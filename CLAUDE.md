## Glossary

The canonical project vocabulary — public types, config keys, CLI/MCP surfaces, rule IDs,
and the planning taxonomy — lives in [`docs/mdlint_v2/glossary.md`](docs/mdlint_v2/glossary.md).
Consult it for term definitions, and keep it current as part of any change that introduces,
renames, or retires a load-bearing term (see the glossary's maintenance rule and the
Repository Hygiene section of `AGENTS.md`). It is a lookup reference, not a precedence tier.
